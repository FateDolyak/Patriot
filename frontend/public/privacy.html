Privacy Policy

The Freedom Trail · Last updated: June 27, 2026

The Freedom Trail ("the App", "we", "us") is a private, invitation-based web application used to run a Fourth of July party game in which guests complete challenges and appear on a leaderboard. The App is operated by an individual host and is available at https://patriot.dolyak.com. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. If you have questions, contact us at admin@dolyak.com.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When you create an account with an email and password, we collect your email address. If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, your basic profile information (such as your name), and your Google account identifier.
  • Profile information. A display name that you choose and can change at any time. This name is shown to other participants on the leaderboard.
  • Activity information. The challenges you mark complete, trivia completions, peer-verification requests and confirmations, and the times of those actions, used to calculate your progress and leaderboard standing.
  • Technical information. Standard server and security logs (such as IP address and request timestamps) generated by our hosting and authentication providers to operate and secure the service.

How we use information

  • To create and authenticate your account and keep you signed in.
  • To record your challenge progress and display the leaderboard (your chosen display name is visible to other participants).
  • To operate, maintain, secure, and troubleshoot the App.

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information.

Google user data — Limited Use. The App's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements. We use Google sign-in solely to authenticate you and create your account. We request only the openid, email, and profile scopes, and we do not use Google user data for any purpose other than providing and improving the App's sign-in and leaderboard features.

How information is stored and shared

Your information is stored using Amazon Web Services (Amazon Cognito for authentication and Amazon DynamoDB for app data) in the United States, protected by encryption in transit and at rest. We share information only with the service providers that make the App work:

  • Amazon Web Services — hosting, authentication, and database.
  • Google — only if you choose to sign in with Google.

Your display name and challenge progress are visible to other participants of the event through the leaderboard. We may also disclose information if required by law.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your information for as long as your account exists or as needed to run the event. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing admin@dolyak.com. You can change your display name yourself from your profile page. After the event, the host may delete the underlying data and infrastructure, which removes all stored personal information.

Your choices and rights

  • Access, update, or delete your information by contacting us.
  • Edit your display name at any time from your profile.
  • Revoke the App's access to your Google account from your Google Account permissions page.

Children's privacy

The App is intended for guests of a private event and is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions or requests regarding this policy or your data: admin@dolyak.com.
